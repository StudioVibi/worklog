About Worklog

Worklog is a lightweight activity log used for visibility, billing, and historical context. When you choose to work, keep this tab open.

Every hour you will hear a beep and a modal will ask for a short description of what you did. Press Register (or Ctrl/Cmd+Enter) to save it. If you forgot the tab open, press Cancel to discard that hour.

You can pause/resume at any time. Closing the tab ends the session.

This is not a time clock. There are no mandatory schedules, minimum hours, or penalties. It is a voluntary log for freelancers/PJ billing and transparency, not a requirement to be online at specific times.
